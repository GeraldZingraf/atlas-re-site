Setup guide

Meet your team in about 4 minutes.

You are not coding. You are hiring a team.

Claude Code is just the office your team works in. It installs like Zoom, takes about four minutes, and you will never see or write a line of code. Follow these steps once and your team is ready.

If the word "code" makes you nervous, relax. You will type plain English to Atlas, the same way you would text an assistant. The setup below is the only technical part, and it is mostly clicking Next.
A short walkthrough video lands here. The written steps below are the full guide on their own.
I do not have my starter yet

  1. Install Claude Code

    Go to claude.com/claude-code and download it for your computer. Open the installer and click through it like any normal app. Sign in or create a free Claude account when it asks.

  2. Open the folder we sent you

    Your starter arrived as a folder. Save it somewhere easy, like your Desktop. In Claude Code, choose "Open folder" and pick that folder. That is your office.

  3. Say hi to Atlas

    In the message box, type hi and press enter. Atlas introduces itself and asks you a few quick questions about you and your business. Answer in plain English. This is how your team learns your voice.

  4. Put your first hire to work

    Paste in a property or a new lead and ask for a listing write-up or a first message. You just got your first piece of work back, in your voice. That is activation. You are running an AI team.

Do I need to know how to code?

No. You type plain English. The setup is the only technical part and it is just installing an app and opening a folder.

Is my information safe?

Your team runs on your own computer. Your business details live in your folder, not on our servers. We keep only your email and license number so we can send your kit and support it.

What if I get stuck?

Reply to your welcome email, or write to atlas@agent-atlas.co. Real person, same day.

What do the first 3 hires actually do?

Atlas is your chief of staff for anything real estate. The second writes your listings and marketing. The third drafts your first message to a new lead. You run them by hand. The full team runs itself and plugs into your tools.
