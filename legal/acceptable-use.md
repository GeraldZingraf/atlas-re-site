## 3. Email and anti-spam (CAN-SPAM)

When you use the Service to draft or send email, you are the sender and you are
responsible for compliance with the CAN-SPAM Act and similar laws. You agree that
every commercial message you send through the Service will:

- Use accurate "From," "To," and routing information that identifies you as the
  sender.
- Use a subject line that is not deceptive.
- Identify the message as an advertisement where required.
- Include your valid physical postal address.
- Include a clear and working way to opt out of future messages, and you will honor
  opt-out requests promptly, within the time the law requires.

You agree not to use the Service to send unsolicited bulk email, to email purchased
or harvested lists, or to email anyone who has opted out or asked you to stop. You
are responsible for maintaining your own suppression and unsubscribe records.

The Service communicates by email and in-app only. It does not send SMS or text

text the people you manage in the Service (for example using contact details the
Service surfaces to you), you are the caller or sender, and you are responsible for
compliance with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) and similar laws,
including:

- Obtaining the level of consent the law requires before contacting someone,
  including any prior express written consent needed for certain messages.
- Honoring "stop" and do-not-contact requests promptly.
- Respecting do-not-call lists and applicable time-of-day restrictions.
