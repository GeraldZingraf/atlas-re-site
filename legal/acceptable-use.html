  • Access or connect any account you are not authorized to use, or access data you do not have the right to access.

2. Real estate advertising and fair housing

You are responsible for ensuring that any marketing, listing content, or advertising created with the Service complies with applicable rules, including:

  • Required advertising disclosures, brokerage identification, and license display requirements in the jurisdictions where you operate.

The Service is a drafting aid. It does not review your content for legal compliance, and you must review and correct anything before you publish or send it.

3. Email and anti-spam (CAN-SPAM)

When you use the Service to draft or send email, you are the sender and you are responsible for compliance with the CAN-SPAM Act and similar laws. You agree that every commercial message you send through the Service will:

  • Use accurate "From," "To," and routing information that identifies you as the sender.
  • Use a subject line that is not deceptive.
  • Identify the message as an advertisement where required.
  • Include your valid physical postal address.
  • Include a clear and working way to opt out of future messages, and you will honor opt-out requests promptly, within the time the law requires.

You agree not to use the Service to send unsolicited bulk email, to email purchased or harvested lists, or to email anyone who has opted out or asked you to stop. You are responsible for maintaining your own suppression and unsubscribe records.

4. No SMS or calling; your own calls and texts (TCPA)

The Service communicates by email and in-app only. It does not send SMS or text messages and does not place phone calls on your behalf. If you separately call or text the people you manage in the Service (for example using contact details the Service surfaces to you), you are the caller or sender, and you are responsible for compliance with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) and similar laws, including:

  • Obtaining the level of consent the law requires before contacting someone, including any prior express written consent needed for certain messages.
  • Honoring "stop" and do-not-contact requests promptly.
  • Respecting do-not-call lists and applicable time-of-day restrictions.

You represent that you have a lawful basis and any required consent to contact the people you reach through the Service, and that the contact data you use (including data in your connected accounts) was obtained lawfully and may be used for the purpose you are using it.
